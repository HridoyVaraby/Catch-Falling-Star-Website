Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Catch Falling Star. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile game.

Last Updated: October 15, 2023

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and profile information.
  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, including device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Game Play Information: We collect data about your game progress, scores, achievements, and in-game purchases.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our game, including the features you use and the time spent playing.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and maintaining our game services
  • Improving and personalizing your gaming experience
  • Processing your in-game transactions
  • Sending you updates, notifications, and promotional materials
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance game features
  • Detecting and preventing fraudulent activities

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us provide and improve our services.
  • Analytics Partners: Companies that help us understand how players use our game.
  • Advertising Partners: Companies that help us deliver relevant advertisements.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Disable analytics collection through device settings

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@catchfallingstar.com

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our game is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
