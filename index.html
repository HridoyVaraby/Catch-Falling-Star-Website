Catch Falling Star

A fun and challenging game where you catch falling stars while avoiding debris. Collect stars to increase your score and hearts to gain extra lives!

Game Features

Progressive Difficulty

The game gets progressively harder as you play, keeping you challenged and engaged!

Multiple Star Types

Collect different types of stars with varying point values to maximize your score!

High Score Tracking

Compete with yourself and others by tracking your highest scores!

How to Play

1

Control the Basket

Use mouse or arrow keys to move the basket left and right to catch falling objects.

2

Catch Stars

Catch falling stars to increase your score. Different stars have different point values!

3

Avoid Debris

Avoid catching debris that reduces your score and can cost you lives.

4

Collect Hearts

Collect hearts to gain extra lives and play longer!
